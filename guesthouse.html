Guest Room Guidelines & Policies

Reservations

To reserve a guest room, please fill out this form.

Confirmation

Once your request has been received, you will receive a confirmation email from cbtabookings@gmail.com with details.

Cancellation

If you need to cancel your reservation, please notify us as soon as possible so we can accommodate other guests.

Meals

If you plan to dine at Telluride, please note any dietary preferences or restrictions in the form.

Clean-up

We kindly encourage guests to tidy up after their stay.

Keys

Before you leave, please ensure you return the keys to avoid inconvenience.

Smoking Policy

Smoking is strictly prohibited in all guest rooms, and common areas.

Contact: For any inquiries, please email cbtabookings@gmail.com
